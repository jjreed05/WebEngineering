Subject: Presenting Information

Guideline

In this website, I made changes in the architecture of presenting information. The first version of the website did not have a format that followed normal web conventions. The original had the body stretched out from one side of the page to the other. In this version, I added left and right columns to focus the main body in the center.


Subject: Navigation

Guidline

I followed the conventions for providing a sense of a landmark in the website. I utilized the left hand hand column to tell the user which page they are on. Additionally, I did have a navigation bar in my original website. The left hand column and the navigation bar allow a better sense of navigation.


Subject: Graphics

Guidline

I wanted to add a graphic to give the website a sense of identity, but I did not want the graphic to take away from the original design. So, I added a peace dollar picture to the right side bar for both pages to exemplify the token of the business.
