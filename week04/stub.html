About us

Our signature coin that we collect is the Peace Dollar. The Peace Dollar is a United States dollar coin minted from 1921 to 1928, and again in 1934 to 1935. Designed by Anthony de Francisci, the coin was the result of a competition to find designs emblematic to peace. Along with our inventory of Peace Dollars, we do sell other coins. Although we have the priority to find Peace Dollars, we will occasionally pick up other vintage coins. Please contact us for more information about our inventory.

The Owner

Peace Dollar Coins is solely operated by Casey Bennet. Mr. Bennet started collecting his first Peace Dollar back in 2012. He then started to obtain a collection of Peace Dollars. In 2014, he decided to start selling his Peace Dollar coins to the world after learning the Coin trading market. Mr. Bennett travels to various coin shows around the Utah valley. He does most of his trading through the internet. Feel free to check out his Instagram page @peace_dollar88 for updates.
